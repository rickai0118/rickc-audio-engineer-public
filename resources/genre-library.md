# 🎵 Suno AI 常用曲风库 (Genre Library)

> 本库按大类 → 子风格 → DAW参数块预设的结构组织。选择曲风后可直接复制对应的 `[GENRE]` 参数块使用。

---

## 1. 流行 (Pop)

### 1.1 华语流行 (C-Pop)

```text
[GENRE]
primary: modern C-Pop with polished vocal-driven production
fusion: light R&B influences and subtle electronic textures
era: 2020s contemporary Chinese pop
tempo: 100-120 BPM mid-tempo groove
key: C major or A minor
```

**典型特征**：旋律优先、人声清晰突出、编曲干净、副歌强 hook  
**代表参考**：周杰伦式R&B旋律 / 邓紫棋式力量流行 / 毛不易式民谣流行

### 1.2 K-Pop

```text
[GENRE]
primary: K-Pop with tight choreography-ready production
fusion: EDM drops and hip-hop verses and R&B bridges
era: 2020s modern K-Pop
tempo: 110-130 BPM energetic and dynamic
key: varies (frequently Bm or Em)
```

**典型特征**：多段式结构（rap verse + sung chorus + dance break）、强节奏、精密制作  

### 1.3 J-Pop

```text
[GENRE]
primary: J-Pop with melodic complexity and emotional depth
fusion: anime-influenced orchestral touches and rock energy
era: modern J-Pop with 2000s nostalgia
tempo: 120-160 BPM (often faster than Western pop)
key: varies (common: F major / Dm / Am)
```

**典型特征**：旋律转折多、结构复杂（A-B-C式）、情感浓烈、常用大段弦乐  

### 1.4 J-Pop Slow Ballad（动漫 ED 风格）

```text
[GENRE]
primary: slow melancholic J-Pop ballad with anime ending theme grandeur
fusion: orchestral strings and modern electronic pop and ambient textures
era: 2024 modern J-Pop with cinematic anime soundtrack influence
tempo: 75-82 BPM slow deliberate mid-tempo with spacious half-time feel
key: C major or E minor
```

**典型特征**：静→动动态对比、日英混合歌词、成员接力唱法、副歌爆发弦乐  
**Exclude 建议**：`upbeat happy cheerful fast energetic dance`

### 1.5 欧美流行 (Western Pop)

```text
[GENRE]
primary: Western pop with radio-ready production
fusion: tropical house elements and EDM-lite drops
era: 2020s streaming-era pop
tempo: 100-125 BPM
key: C major / G major (bright) or Am / Em (moody)
```

### 1.6 Indie Pop / 独立流行

```text
[GENRE]
primary: indie pop with lo-fi charm and organic textures
fusion: folk acoustic elements and dreampop atmospherics
era: 2020s indie with vintage warmth
tempo: 90-115 BPM relaxed groove
key: D major or G major (warm open keys)
```

## 3. 电子 (Electronic)

### 3.1 Melodic Future Bass

```text
[GENRE]
primary: Melodic Future Bass with Chillstep and Dreamy Synth-pop textures
fusion: cinematic emotional arc and lush supersaw chords and ambient pads
era: 2024 modern future bass
tempo: 100-110 BPM with half-time feel (50-55 BPM feel)
key: A minor or F major
```

**适配场景**：男女对唱 / 宇宙感 / 情感爆发

### 3.2 电子流行 (Synthpop / Electropop)

```text
[GENRE]
primary: synthpop with retro-futuristic synth textures
fusion: new wave and modern electronic production
era: 80s inspired with modern polish
tempo: 118-128 BPM
key: Cm or Am (dark synthwave feel)
```

### 3.3 House / Deep House

```text
[GENRE]
primary: deep house with warm bass-driven groove
fusion: soulful vocal chops and jazzy chord progressions
era: modern deep house
tempo: 120-124 BPM four-on-the-floor
key: Gm or Dm
```

### 3.4 Lo-fi Hip Hop / Chillhop

```text
[GENRE]
primary: lo-fi hip hop with warm dusty beats
fusion: jazz samples and vinyl crackle and tape saturation
era: modern lo-fi aesthetic
tempo: 70-85 BPM head-nodding groove
key: F major or C major (mellow warm)
```

### 3.5 Melodic Phonk（Drift Phonk）

```text
[GENRE]
primary: Melodic Phonk with ethereal dreamy atmosphere
fusion: dark phonk bass and atmospheric pads and emotional melody
era: 2024 modern melodic phonk
tempo: 138-142 BPM with punchy 808 bass and skittering trap hi-hats
key: A minor or D minor
```

**Exclude 建议**：`aggressive and harsh and noisy and lo-fi distortion`

### 3.6 Future Bass

```text
[GENRE]
primary: future bass with lush supersaws and heavy drops
fusion: pop vocal sensibility and trap percussion
era: 2020s future bass
tempo: 140-150 BPM (half-time feel)
key: F major or Ab major
```

### 3.7 Ambient / Atmospheric

```text
[GENRE]
primary: ambient electronic with expansive soundscapes
fusion: drone textures and granular synthesis and field recordings
era: modern ambient
tempo: 60-80 BPM or free tempo
key: open/modal (Dorian or Aeolian)
```
